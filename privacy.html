Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your information

Effective Date: 2025

At UPSKILL, accessible from upskill.com, your privacy is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by us and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information (e.g., Name, Email Address, Phone Number)
  • Usage Data (e.g., pages viewed, time spent on site, clicks)
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Improving your user experience
  • Communicating with you regarding updates, promotions, or support
  • Analyzing website usage to improve functionality

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand your preferences. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may share your data with trusted third-party services to support our operations, such as:

  • Email marketing platforms
  • Payment processors

Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • File a complaint with a regulatory authority

Children's Information

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided such data, please contact us, and we will take the necessary steps to remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review this page periodically for any changes.
